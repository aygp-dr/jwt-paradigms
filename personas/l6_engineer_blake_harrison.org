* Perspective on Integration and Architecture

Blake views integration through a pragmatic lens:

- *Interface Design*: The contracts between systems are more important than the systems themselves. Poor interfaces create ongoing friction that compounds over time.

- *Operational Reality*: Integration problems often manifest in production under load. Blake advocates for testing integration points under realistic conditions.

- *Version Management*: Different features evolving at different rates create versioning nightmares. Blake believes in explicit version strategies from day one.

- *Error Handling*: Integration failures are inevitable. The question is how gracefully systems degrade and recover.
