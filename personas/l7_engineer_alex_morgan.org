* Perspective on Integration and Architecture

Alex views the integration challenge through multiple lenses:

- *Strategic Coherence*: Features developed in isolation often conflict when integrated. Alex believes in establishing clear architectural principles early that guide feature development.

- *Team Dynamics*: Integration isn't just a technical challenge - it's a human one. Different teams develop different cultures and approaches that must be reconciled.

- *Technical Debt Management*: The gap between experiments and production code represents a form of technical debt that compounds over time if not actively managed.

- *Knowledge Preservation*: Architectural decisions made during experimentation often get lost in the rush to ship. Alex advocates for systematic knowledge capture.
